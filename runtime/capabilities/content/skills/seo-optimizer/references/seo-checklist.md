# SEO Checklist

Compare each item against `audit-seo.sh` output before recommending edits.

1. **Single H1** — exactly one; primary keyword present.
2. **H2 hierarchy** — at least 2 H2s for scannability; no skipped levels (no H4 without H3).
3. **Title tag** — ≤60 characters, includes primary keyword near the front.
4. **Meta description** — ≤155 characters, action-oriented, includes primary keyword once.
5. **Primary keyword density** — present in H1, first paragraph, and at least one H2; do not stuff.
6. **Image alt text** — every `![...]` has descriptive alt that includes a relevant secondary keyword when natural.
7. **Internal links** — at least one to a related canonical doc; anchor text describes the destination.
8. **URL slug** — kebab-case, primary keyword first, no stop words.

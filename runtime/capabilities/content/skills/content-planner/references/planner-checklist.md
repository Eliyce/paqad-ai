# Brief Checklist

Every brief must contain:

1. **Audience** — a specific reader or role, not "everyone".
2. **Goal** — one observable outcome (book a call, fix a config, decide on X).
3. **Constraints** — channel, length, tone, brand rules, regulatory limits.
4. **Outline** — section list with each section's purpose.
5. **Dependencies** — what blocks the draft (SME interview, legal review, screenshots).
6. **Success metric** — how the team will know the piece worked.

Mark unknowns explicitly (e.g. `Audience: unknown — pending product input`) rather than guessing.

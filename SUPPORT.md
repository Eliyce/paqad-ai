# Getting help with paqad-ai

The right place to ask depends on what you need.

## I have a question or want to discuss an idea

Use [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/Eliyce/paqad-ai/discussions).
Discussions are the best place for:

- "How do I use feature X with stack Y?"
- "Is paqad-ai the right tool for my use case?"
- "I have a design idea I want feedback on."
- "Show and tell — here's how I'm using paqad-ai."

## I found a bug

Open a [bug report issue](https://github.com/Eliyce/paqad-ai/issues/new/choose).
Include:

- The output of `paqad-ai doctor`.
- Your `paqad-ai --version` and `node --version`.
- A minimal reproduction.

## I want a new feature

Small, well-defined requests: open a
[feature request issue](https://github.com/Eliyce/paqad-ai/issues/new/choose).

Open-ended ideas or "wouldn't it be cool if..." conversations: open a
[Discussion](https://github.com/Eliyce/paqad-ai/discussions) first.
This avoids issues sitting open for months with no path forward.

## I have a security concern

**Do not open a public issue.** Follow the process in [`SECURITY.md`](./SECURITY.md).
